SPRITE SUPPORTS
BLACK LIVES MATTER
Black lives matter.
We stand with the Black community
including our creators, collaborators, employees and families.
We stand with everyone who is fighting for racial justice.
For far too long, America has loved Black culture.
But not Black people.
Our hearts are broken at the killings of
George Floyd, Tony McDade, Nina Pop, Breonna Taylor, Ahmaud Arbery, Sandra Bland,
Eric Garner, Tamir Rice, Laquan McDonald, Philando Castile, Michael Brown, Trayvon Martin,
and so many others.
To continue to empower the Black community,
we are donating to Black Lives Matter Global Network
and to our local Atlanta community.
But that’s not enough.
We need more than transactions.
We need transformation.
We are announcing a partnership with Black Lives Matter Global Network.
We are committed to being vocal and active in the fight for racial justice
long after the news cycle has moved on.
We’re in this for the long haul.
It’s not enough to say we’re anti-racism.
We are elevating our commitment to the community and the culture we serve. We are
committed to using our platforms to not only amplify the voices of our community but help them
affect change.
We are who we are because of the Black community.
It’s time to act like it.
More to come.
